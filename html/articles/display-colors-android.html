Adjust the Color Filters

You can apply various filters or adjust color tones on your Android device to meet your needs.


Night Light / Eye Comfort Shield

Soften the screen colors by limiting the blue light and using warmer colors


Google Pixel

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Display & Touch
  3. Tap Night Light
  4. Enable

You can also setup a scheduale to automatically enable during a certain time.


Samsung Galaxy

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Display
  3. Tap Eye Comfort Shield
  4. Enable

You can also setup a scheduale to automatically enable during a certain time.


Color Correction / Color Adjustment

Adjust how colors display on your device.

Google Pixel

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap on Accessibility
  3. Tap Color and Motion
  4. Tap Color Correction
  5. Enable Color Correction
  6. Select the color mode you need.
  7. Adjust the intensity with the slider

You can also setup a shortcut to toggle the color mode on and off.


Samsung Galaxy

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap on Accessibility
  3. Tap Visibility Enhancements
  4. Tap Color and Motion
  5. Tap Color Adjustment
  6. Enable Color Adjustment
  7. Select the color mode you need.
  8. Adjust the intensity with the slider

Color Contrast / High Contrast Fonts

Apply a higher contrast to the display and text to enhance legibility.


Google Pixel

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Display
  3. Color Contrast
  4. Choose a contrast intecity.

You can also Enable Outline Text for even more contrast, which apply a background to text.


Samsung Galaxy

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap on Accessibility
  3. Tap Visibility Enhancements
  4. Tap High Contrast Fonts
  5. Enable High Contrast Fonts

You can also setup a shortcut to toggle High Contrast Fonts on and off.


Color Inversion

Invert the colors so that light colors are dark and vise versa.


Google Pixel

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap Accessibility
  3. Tap Color and Motion
  4. Tap Color Inversion
  5. Enable Color Inversion

You can also setup a shortcut to toggle Color Inersion on and off.


Samsung Galaxy

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap Accessibility
  3. Tap Visibility Enhancements
  4. Tap Color Inversion
  5. Enable Color Inversion

You can also setup a shortcut to toggle Color Inersion on and off.

Extra Dim (Google Pixel only)

Dim the display darker than the typical brightness slider allows.

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap on Accessibility
  3. Tap Extra Dim
  4. Enable Extra Dim

You can also setup a shortcut to toggle Extra Dim on and off.

Color Filter (Samsung Galaxy only)

Apply a color tint to the display.

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap Accessibility
  3. Tap Visibility Enhancements
  4. Tap Add Color Filter
  5. Select a to tint the display to
  6. Enable Color Filter
  7. Adjust the opacity of the filter

You can also setup a shortcut to toggle the color filter on and off.
