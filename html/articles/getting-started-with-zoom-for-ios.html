Getting Started with the Accessibility Zoom Feature for iOS, iPadOS, and iPodOS

The Zoom feature on iOS, iPadOS, and iPodOS allows a user to magnify anything on your display no matter what you are viewing. You can enlarge the display entirely or in a resizable window. This article will go over how to setup, enable, use, and disable Zoom.

Locate and Enable

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Accessibility
  3. Tap Zoom
  4. Enable Zoom

Use Zoom

  • Double-tap the screen with three figners to zoom in and out
  • Double-tap and swipe up and down with three fingers to adjust the magnification or triple-tap with three fingers to bring up the quick settings, then slide the slider to adjust the magnification.
  • Drag with three fingers around the screen to move the magnification window around.

Zoom Settings

There are many settings you can adjust for Zoom listed and explained below:

  • Follow Focus: Track your selections, text editor cursor, and typing.
  • Smart Typing: Switch to Window Zoom when a keyboard appears.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: Control Zoom using an external keyboard with shortcuts.
  • Zoom Controller: Control Zoom with an on-screen controller that can be moved and placed anywhere on the screen at any time.
  • Zoom Region: Choose Full Screen Zoom or Window Zoom.
  • Zoom Filter: Apply one of the following filters: Inverted, Grayscale: Grayscale Inverted: Low Light (dims the display in Full Screen Zoom or the window in Window Zoom) or choose none.
  • Show while Mirroring: If enabled, whenever you use Zoom while sharing your screen, viewers will see it. If disabled, viewers will not seee you using Zoom during screen mirroring.
  • Maximum Zoom Level: Adjust the max magnification you can perform with Zoom.

Use Zoom with Pointer Device

If you use a pointer device, You can use zoom with the following settings found in Pointer Control section under Accessibility in Settings.

  • Zoom Pan: Choose Continuous- Centered- or Edges to set how the screen image moves with the pointer.
  • Adjust Size with Zoom: Allow the pointer to scale with zoom.

Zoom Controller

  • Tap the controller once to show the Zoom Menu
  • Double-tap the controller to quickly zoom in and out
  • Tap and hold, then drag the controller to temporarily zoom in. Release to zoom out.
  • Drag the controller while zoomed in to pan around and move the zoom window (both Full Screen Zoom and Window Zoom)
  • Drag the controller while zoomed out to move it.
