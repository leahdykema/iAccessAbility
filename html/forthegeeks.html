Leah Dykema's Apple Geek Apps

MacTracker - App Store: Free - The ultimate Apple product archive, packed with every detail you could ever want. From past to present, products are meticulously listed by category and model, complete with technical specs and historical info. If you keep this on your Home Screen, you're a true Apple geek.

Facades - App Store: Free - A complete archive of Apple Stores, featuring locations, storefront photos, history, and more! Browse past and present stores with major historical milestones, including renovations, relocations, anniversaries, and closures. If this has a prime spot on your Home Screen, you're a certified Apple geek.

LocalSend - App Store: Free - An easy way to transfer files between iOS and Android for free, perfect for tech nerds with devices from both worlds.
